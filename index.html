My Future Career Plans

My future career plans are to go to PA school after graduation, however preparation to do so starts now. To apply for PA school I need 1500+ hours of patient care which I plan to start obtaining my junior year. Along with that I have to get volunteer hours which I plan to do at Prisma Health in Columbia. I also plan to shadow at least two PAs sometime soon to see what a general work day looks like.... I decided PA school was what I wanted to do because I have loved being around children growing up, so I hope to specialize in pediatrics. I plan to apply to many schools to better my chances of getting in since it is a highly competitive program. I plan to apply to schools in South Carolina, North Carolina, and Florida. After finishing PA school I will look for jobs in South Carolina as well as North Carolina because I want to be somewhat close to home.
